Discover Ceylon Gems: Our Story, Vision, and Values


CeylonGems is one of Sri Lanka's leading exporters of coloured gemstones. The company was founded in 2016 but has over 10 years of experience in the natural gemstone trade, especially sapphires and spinels.



The company specializes in high-end niche markets of natural-colored gemstones. The product line consists primarily of faceted unheated free-size sapphires of all grades, especially medium to high-grade large-sized sapphires. The sapphire range includes blue, pink, yellow sapphire, pastel sapphire, ruby, star sapphire, and star ruby. In addition, the company has a large selection of other important gemstones such as Spinel, Tsavorites, Tourmaline and Zircons. CeylonGems has a reputation for having one of the finest collections of natural gemstones in Sri Lanka, if not the world. examine gemstone

   

CeylonGems operates its own gem-cutting and polishing (lapidary) manufacturing unit. Additionally, our company only provides stones with confirmed certifications from well-equipped gem testing labs to ensure the quality of its products before putting the stones out to the market. Our markets span multiple regions including America, Europe, and Asia. Europe is our main market, with customers in United State of America, Sweden, Australia, and the United Kingdom. In Asia, we export to Singapore and Thailand.
